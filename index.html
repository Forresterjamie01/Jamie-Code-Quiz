Rules of the Game
You will have 60 seconds to take this quiz. When you begin the timer will start and you will be presented with the first question. For each question you answer incorrectly, time will be substracted from the clock. When all questions are answered or the time reaches 0, the game is over. Once the game is over, you will be prompted to sign with your initials and you will recieve your score.
